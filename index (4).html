📢 BISP 8171 Eligibility Instructions:

💡 Method 1: Check Via SMS

Send your National Identity Card Number (CNIC) without spaces or hyphens to 8171 from any registered cellular SIM card. You will receive an immediate SMS containing your active payout amount and center details.

🌐 Method 2: Check Web Portal

Go to BISP official web portal: https://8171.bisp.gov.pk. Provide CNIC and enter verification code shown on screen to confirm status.
